Features

The algorithm aims to find similarities in the provided songs and emphasizes those when providing the suggestions.

Unlike other programs who claim to provide a similar experience, we compare your songs to the entire Spotify catelog rather than a small list of preferred songs.

Why us

As people from musical backgrounds, we understand how hard it is to come by good music sometimes, and how it feels to settle on tracks rather than loving them.

Our goal is to reduce these types of experiences and ensure the best listening environment for our users.
